Easybank landing page

I developed a landing page to a high degree of accuracy, taking into consideration the design specifications provided in a PDF format instead of a Figma file. To achieve this, I used Remix and tailwindCSS, both of which have garnered favorable reviews, while remaining within my familiar realm of expertise. The resultant design is responsive and optimized for mobile screens with a width of approximately 760 pixels, as well as desktop screens with a width of roughly 1440 pixels. Although there may be minor discrepancies in certain intermediate screen sizes, the overall integrity of the design has been maintained.
